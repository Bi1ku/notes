Engel v. Vitale

Facts:
  - 1962
  - NY Board of Regents had an optional prayer recited after pledge

Engel said that it violated the Establishment Clause of the Constitution.

Decision:
  - (6-1 decision) Yes. It did violate the establishment cluase.

Why it matters:
  - Ruled in favor of individual liberties (libertives v. social order)
  - Laid groundwork for many subsequent cases in schools and religious
  activities
