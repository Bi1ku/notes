McDonald v. Chicago

Facts:
  - Heller v. District of Columbia => restrictive gun ownership laws in dc are
  unconstitutional. Since dc is a federal district, however, only applies to federal.
  - McDonald wanted ot apply this to the states
    - Hunter who lived in gang infested area. Robbed 5 times already
    - Wanted handgun, but chicago laws made it almost impossible legally
  - McDonald argued that second amendment was violated.
  - Chicago argbued that these gun laws were necessary to uphold public safety.

Decesions:
  - Ruled in favor of McDonald.
    - Expanded Heller v. District of Columbia to states

Why it matters?:
  - Selective Incorporation
  - Other states had to rewrite gun laws
