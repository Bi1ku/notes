Holding the Bureaucracy Accountable

Congress is concered and wants to make sure that the bnureaucracies are implementing
and enforcing the laws as Congress intended.
  - To excercise this oversight:
    - Committee hearings
    - Power of the Purse (power to fund these agencies)

President:
  - Appointments (except independent regulatory heads)
    - Fire and hire
  - Executive Orders to bring them in line
  - Compliance Monitoring

Judicial:
