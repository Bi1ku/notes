Political Socialization

How do people develop into one political ideology over the other?

Political Socialization:
  - Family
  - Schools
  - Peers
  - Media
  - Civic/Religious Organizations
    - E.g., Boy Scouts (encourages patriotism)
    - E.g., Liberal/Conservative Mosque, Liberal/Conservative Church, etc.
  - Globalization
    - Inter-connectedness throughout the word through economic and social ties
