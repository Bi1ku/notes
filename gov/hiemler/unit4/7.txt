Ideologies of Political Parties

Political ideology - set of ideas that form the basis for decision making.

Republicans leans conservative (obviously)

    - Independence
    - Tough on crime
    - Marraiges and children. Traditional family structure..
    - etc.

Democratic leans liberal (obviously)
  - Goals based in this
    - Level economic playing field
    - Ending poverty
    - Breaking up monopolies

    - Women's rights, LGBTQ, Tribal, etc.
    - "Diversity is our greatest strength."
