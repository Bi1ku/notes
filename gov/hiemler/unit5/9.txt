Congressional Elections

Congressional Elections
  - Every two year
  - House have two year terms
  - Senate six year terms
    - 1/3 up for reelection every two years

Midterm elections are congressional elections that happen in the middle
between presidential elections.

Incumbency Advantage
  - Even more pronounced than presidency 
  - 90% are reelected
  - Why?
    - Name recognition
    - Track record
    - Established funding
    - Safe districts (thanks to gerrymandering)

Candidates for each party are chosen by primary elections.
